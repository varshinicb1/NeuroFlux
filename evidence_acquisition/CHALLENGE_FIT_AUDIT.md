# CHALLENGE FIT AUDIT
## Strategic Evaluation of All Opportunity Directions

**Objective:** Determine if project is drifting from original challenge  
**Original Challenge:** "Discover patentable AFPM aerospace starter-generator"  
**Date:** May 31, 2026

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Key Finding:** The project has **drifted significantly** from the original challenge. The pain point analysis identified **high-value opportunities that VIOLATE** one or more challenge requirements (AFPM, Aerospace, Starter-generator, Patentable).

**Critical Discovery:** The **highest commercial value opportunities** (self-monitoring HV insulation, digital certification) are **NOT AFPM machines** - they are **system-level innovations**.

**Challenge Compliance:** Only **3 of 8 opportunities** satisfy ALL challenge requirements:
- ✓ A5 YASA
- ✓ A7 High-Speed AFPM + Gearbox  
- ✓ A9 Modular Fault-Tolerant AFPM

**The other 5 opportunities VIOLATE the AFPM requirement** but offer higher commercial value.
