## PART 5: MINIMUM INVENTION SPECIFICATION

### The Winning Delta: YASA + FOSM

**Standard YASA (Baseline):**
- Yokeless and segmented armature topology
- 6-12 segments
- Standard windings
- Expired patents = freedom to operate
- Commodity status

**Minimum Invention Addition:**

1. **FBG Sensor Array (12 sensors):**
   - 2 sensors per segment (6 segments)
   - Embedded in copper winding
   - Distributed along winding length
   - Temperature and strain capable

2. **Fiber Routing:**
   - Fibers routed through stator structure
   - Strain relief at segment boundaries
   - Exit to stationary interrogator

3. **Interrogator Unit:**
   - Off-the-shelf FBG interrogator
   - Signal processing for temperature/strain discrimination
   - Calibration per machine

4. **Structural Integration:**
   - Slots in winding form for fiber
   - Protection from winding insertion forces
   - Thermal interface matching winding

**What Makes This Patentable:**
- Per-segment FBG distribution NOT in prior art
- Siemens (DE10139096A1): "distributed" along winding (radial flux)
- Our invention: "distributed" per segment (axial flux segmented)
- Simultaneous temp + strain (Siemens eliminates strain)

**Evidence:** @/PRIOR_ART_FORENSICS_REPORT.md:300-320
> "Siemens teaches 'distributed' along winding length, NOT per-segment distribution"
